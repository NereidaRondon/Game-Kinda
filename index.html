Kinda

The word game

(Because it's kinda like Wordle.)

Game Rules:

Guess a 5 letter word

You will get 5 additional guesses per round

Even if your word doesn't match, letters from your guess that match the letters in the mystery word AND are in the correct spot will remain in place

Hints:

There are 200 different words, from A to Zesty

No plural, 4 letter words here
for example: cats, words, lists

The words won't be an animal or a fruit
