Kinda, the word game

(Because it's kinda like Wordle.)


Game Rules:

  1. Guess a 5 letter word
  2. You will get 5 guesses per round
  3. There are 200 different words, from A to Zesty
  4. If your word is not a match, then you try again until you're out of guesses
  5. Even if your word doesn't match, letters in your word may match and remain in place
  6. No plural 4 letter words here
  7. The words won't be an animal or a fruit
