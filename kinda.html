The word game

(Because it's kinda like Wordle.)


Game Rules:

  1. Guess a 5 letter word
  2. You will get 5 additional guesses per round
  3. There are 200 different words, from A to Zesty
  4. If your word is not a match, then you try again until you're out of guesses
  5. Even if your word doesn't match, letters from your guess that match the letters in the mystery word AND are in the correct spot will remain in place
Hints:
  • No plural, 4 letter words here
    for example: cats, words, lists
  • The words won't be an animal or a fruit
