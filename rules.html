Game Rules:


Guess a 5 letter word, then submit to check if you guessed correctly

You will have 5 additional guesses per round

Letters in the correct place will remain in place

Used letters not in the word will be removed from the QWERTY list

Hints:


There are 200 different words, from A to Zesty

No plural, 4 letter words here
for example: cats, words, lists

The words won't be an animal or a fruit
