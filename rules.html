Game Rules:


Guess a 5 letter word, then hit submit. Each game is 10 rounds.

Each round will provide 6 chances to guess the word.

After each guess, letters in the correct place will remain in place. Letters in the word but not in the correct place will turn black.

Already used letters not found in the word will be removed from the QWERTY list

Hints:


There are 200 different words, from A to Zesty

No plural, 4 letter words here
for example: cats, words, lists

The words won't be an animal or a fruit
